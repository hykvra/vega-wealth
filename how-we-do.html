Meet an adviser

Your Advice Journey

Wealth. It's Personal.

Your life choices are as unique as you are, and so is our approach to financial advice. Our tailored approach is centred on uncovering what truly matters to you, helping you build and preserve wealth while shaping the life experiences that hold the greatest meaning. We guide you towards clear, confident decisions, giving you the freedom to create and enjoy the life you aspire to. And as your life evolves, we're right here with you — ensuring your strategy keeps pace so you always feel supported, informed, and on the right path.

Our Advice Roadmap

Intro Call

Your journey begins with a simple 15-minute conversation. We'll explore what matters most to you, including your goals, your priorities, and the areas you'd like to focus on. We'll also introduce you to Vega Wealth and walk you through our advice process, so you feel comfortable, informed, and confident from the very start.

Ongoing Partnership

90 Day Check-in

The first 90 days are an important milestone. We check in to make sure everything is progressing as planned, walk you through progress to date, and address anything on your mind. It's our way of making sure you feel informed, supported, and confident as you settle into the early stages of your journey.

Mid-Year Portfolio Review

Your mid-year review is a dedicated opportunity to look at how your portfolio is performing. Through proactive monitoring and careful evaluation, we ensure your investments remain aligned with your goals, preferences, and risk tolerance — so you can stay confident, informed, and on track toward the future you want.

Annual Strategy Meeting

Your annual strategy meeting is our most comprehensive review of the year. We revisit your goals, celebrate your progress, and explore what's possible in the year ahead. Life doesn't always follow the plan, and when it takes an unexpected turn, we're right here to adjust your strategy so it continues to reflect your ideal life and grows with you.

Let's Connect!

During our 15-minute call, we'll explore what matters most to you, your personal and financial goals, and how Vega Wealth can help you get there.
